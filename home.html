Sony

Sony Corporation (stylized as SONY) is a Japanese multinational conglomerate corporation headquartered in K≈çnan, Minato, Tokyo. Its diversified business includes consumer and professional electronics, gaming, entertainment and financial services. The company owns the largest music entertainment business in the world, and is one of the leading manufacturers of electronic products for the consumer and professional markets, and a leading player in the film industry. Sony was ranked 105th on the 2017 list of Fortune Global 500.
